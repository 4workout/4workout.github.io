# 🛡️ Privacy Policy

*Last updated: May 14, 2025*

Welcome to **Warrior’s Path: Train Like a Champion**! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information as you forge your legend.

---

## 🔍 1. What We Collect

We may collect the following information to enhance your training experience:

* **Username** and basic profile info (e.g., gender)
* **Workout activity** (completed workouts, XP gained, levels)
* **Progress stats** (rewards claimed, quests completed)
* Device information (for debugging and analytics)

---

## 🧠 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

* Track your warrior journey (XP, level-ups, achievements)
* Personalize your experience
* Provide and improve app functionality
* Identify and fix bugs

We **do not sell** your data to third parties.

---

## 🔐 3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access control measures. We use trusted third-party services like Firebase to help store and protect your information.

---

## 🤝 4. Third-Party Services

Some features may rely on third-party services, including:

* **Authentication** (e.g., Firebase Auth)
* **Analytics** (e.g., usage stats to improve the app)

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect anonymized data.

You have the right to:

* View the data we collect
* Request deletion of your account and data
* Opt out of non-essential data tracking

Contact us at **[support@warriorspath.app](mailto:support@warriorspath.app)** to make a request.
